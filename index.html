🏥 INPUT — Enteral Nutrition Planning (Εντερική Σίτιση)
ℹ️ This module is a calculation aid for inpatient/enteral feeding plans, based on the ESPEN practical guideline for polymorbid medical inpatients (Clin Nutr 2024;43:674–691). It computes targets and formula math — it does not decide the feeding route (oral/EN/PN) for you. That decision always requires bedside clinical assessment by the treating team, under your supervisor's direction.
1 · MALNUTRITION RISK SCREENING
Pick whichever screening tool your ward uses — MUST and NRS-2002 are both ESPEN-endorsed.
MUST — Malnutrition Universal Screening Tool
NRS-2002 — Nutritional Risk Screening
Age ≥70 adds +1 automatically (from Client Profile age).
EAT-10 — Swallowing / Dysphagia Screen
Each item 0 (no problem) – 4 (severe problem). Directly relevant to the oral-vs-EN route decision.
